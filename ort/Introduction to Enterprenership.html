Home Assignment 2 - Elevator Pitch

EVERY student should prepare an elevator pitch lasting in 3-5 minutes together with his or her team members. Almost everything about ANY business idea should be incorporated into this elevator pitch. The student should make sure that a shark (potential investor) is convinced. 

Minimum requirements to be accepted:

  1. Well prepared and designed presentation (look at this sample).
  2. Maximum duration of the presentation MUST be 5 minutes.
  3. All members of a team MUST turn on their cameras (they all can join in speech or only one of them can deliver the elevator pitch).
  4. Video and audio quality is required.
  5. After recording a Big Blue Button video, submit exactly the followings as a text: the link of the video, its date and time. Please use Microsoft Edge as a browser if you face difficulties in recording. If you cannot record it by yourselves, to learn more watch 
    .
  6. Any appropriate language can be used during the presentation.
